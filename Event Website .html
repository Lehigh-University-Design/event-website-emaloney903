Parking

Parking is available for all Festival-Goers, Overnight or not. Check your ticket upon purchase for your assigned lot and provide your ticket to th parking staff as you enter. Be sure to lock things up - ‘Night in the Woods’ festival and Pinecrest Summer Camp are not responsible for any lost or stolen items.

Food and Drink

From gourmet food trucks offering artisanal delights to cozy campfire gatherings with classic s'mores, the dining options at 'Night in the Woods' promise to elevate your festival experience. Enjoy a diverse array of cuisines from our fleet of food trucks, Available 24/7. Or, grab a bite at the Pinecrest Lodge Dining Hall, where professionally trained chefs elevate traditional camp fare. (9AM-10PM)

Participate in classic summer camp activities. Get in touch with your creative side at our arts and crafts are, join our stargazing sessions and marvel at the beauty of the universe, Try your hand at archery, or even join our expert guides on breathtaking nature hikes. And end every day with exceptional live music and bonfires under the stars.

Cool off and soak up the sun at our pristine lakefront. Swim, kayak, or simply lounge on the beach. The clear waters and serene surroundings provide the perfect setting for unwinding.

Indulge your senses at our festival marketplace, where a curated selection of local artisans and vendors awaits. From handcrafted treasures to delectable treats, each stall tells a unique story. Explore the vibrant tapestry of creativity and flavor, and take home a piece of the Catskills with you.

Safety

Your safety is our priority at "Night in the Woods." Our professional security team is on-site, ensuring a secure environment. Thorough security checks, medical facilities, and emergency contacts are in place. Hydration stations, COVID-19 safety measures, and a respectful atmosphere contribute to a worry-free experience. Enjoy the music and festivities with confidence, knowing we've got your safety ;covered.

Lodging

 

Pinecrest Lodge

If you're looking for a touch of luxury amid the w

ilderness, the Pinecrest Lodge is your sanctuary. These well-appointed lodge rooms offer a comfortable retreat with private bathrooms, ensuring you have a rejuvenating space to rest after a day of festivities.

Rental Cabins

For those seeking a touch of rustic charm with added comfort, our cozy cabins provide the perfect mix. Nestled in the woods, our cabins offer a peaceful haven, featuring bunk beds and basic amenities. It's an excellent option for those who want to experience the festival with a little extra comfort.

Tent Campsites

Embrace the true spirit of the festival by camping under the starlit Catskill sky. Pitch your own tent in our designated camping areas, and wake up to the crisp mountain air. It's a fantastic way to connect with nature and fellow festival-goers.
